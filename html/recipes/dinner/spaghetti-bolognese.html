Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion, garlic, olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Saute onion and garlic in olive oil, then add beef until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Serve sauce over spaghetti.
